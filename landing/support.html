Quick start

  1. Create an account and start your free trial at app.gtmcat.xyz.
  2. Add GTMCat Capture from the Chrome Web Store and pin it.
  3. Pair the extension: open the GTMCat dashboard and click "Connect extension" — pairing is one click (no codes to copy). The browser links to your account.
  4. Log in to reddit.com in that browser (or in a throwaway read-only "capture profile").
  5. Open the side panel and start capture. Scored threads appear in your Signals inbox.

Frequently asked questions

Does GTMCat post or vote for me?
No. GTMCat is strictly read-only and human-in-the-loop. It captures Reddit through your own session and you engage by hand, from your own account. It never posts, comments, votes, or automates anything on Reddit.
Do you store my Reddit password?
Never. The extension uses your existing logged-in browser session. We store your Reddit username only (for the posting identity), never credentials.
Capture isn't running / shows offline.
Make sure (a) the extension is paired, (b) you're logged in to reddit.com in that browser, and (c) the browser/profile is open. The side panel shows per-profile status; "needs a reddit.com login" means that profile's session expired — open reddit.com there and log in.
Why don't I see private/restricted subreddits?
GTMCat only captures what your own session can see, and never adds restricted-sub content to the shared pool. If your session can't see it, GTMCat doesn't fetch it.
How do I export or delete my data?
In the app: Settings → Export (one-click data export) and Delete everything (purges your account data). See the Privacy Policy.
Billing, plans, cancellation, refunds?
See Pricing and the Refund & Cancellation Policy. You can cancel anytime from the app; access continues to the end of the paid period.
I hit a usage limit on the free trial.
The trial has capture/credit limits so it stays free for us to offer. Upgrade in the app to lift them — your existing data and setup carry straight over.
