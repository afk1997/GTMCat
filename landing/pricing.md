# Pricing

Two meters drive pricing, and we show both plainly: **AI credits** (drafts, reports, AEO checks) and
**captured volume** (Reddit threads scored per month). No license keys — your subscription is the
entitlement. Every plan includes **unlimited capture profiles** (throwaway read-only logins).

Start on the **3-day free trial** — no credit card required. You can try the full product with
trial limits; upgrade anytime and your data and setup carry straight over.

|---|---|---|---|---|
| Price | $0 · 3-day trial | **$49**/mo | **$79**/mo | **$249+**/mo |
| For | Kicking the tires | Solo operators | Serious operators | High volume / teams |
| AI credits / mo | 25 | 80 | 200 | 1,000 |
| Captured threads / mo | 500 | 2,000 | 5,000 | 25,000 |
| Watched subreddits | 2 | 5 | 10 | Unlimited |
| Capture profiles | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |
| Buyer-intent scoring | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Compliance read + humanized drafts | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Mentions & competitor intelligence | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |
| Deep research (cited reports) | — | ✓ | ✓ | ✓ |

> Note: the free **trial** runs with lower limits than Pro so it stays genuinely free — it's there
> to show you the product, then you pick the plan that fits.

**What's a credit?** A reply draft costs 1 credit; a deep-research report costs more (it fans out
across your whole corpus). Credits and capture reset monthly.

**Billing.** Plans bill monthly in advance through Dodo Payments (our merchant of record). Cancel
anytime — see the [Refund & Cancellation Policy](./refunds.md). Renewals continue until you cancel.
