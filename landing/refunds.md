# Refund & Cancellation Policy

**Last updated: June 30, 2026**

> ⚠️ Confirm this matches what you actually want to offer (and what Dodo Payments, your merchant of
> record, supports) before publishing. Adjust the window/terms as needed.

GTMCat subscriptions are billed by our merchant of record, **Dodo Payments**, who appears on your
statement and handles the transaction and any applicable taxes.

## Try before you pay
Every plan starts with a **3-day free trial — no credit card required**. We encourage you to use the
trial to decide if GTMCat fits before you ever pay, which is the best way to avoid needing a refund.

## Cancelling
You can cancel anytime from **Settings → Plan** in the app. Cancellation:
- stops all future renewals, and
- keeps your access until the end of the current paid period (we don't cut you off mid-cycle).

After your paid period ends you drop to the free tier; your data is retained per the
[Privacy Policy](./privacy.md), and you can export or delete it anytime.

## Refunds
- **Monthly plans are generally non-refundable** for the current period once it has started, because
  you keep access for the full period after cancelling.
- If you were **charged in error**, charged after cancelling, or hit a billing problem, email us
  within **14 days** of the charge and we'll make it right — including a full refund where warranted.
- We handle refund requests case-by-case in good faith, and always where required by law.

## How to request a refund
Email **[support@gtmcat.xyz]** from your account email with the charge date and the reason. We reply
within 1–2 business days. Approved refunds are issued to your original payment method via Dodo
Payments (timing depends on your bank/card issuer).

## Chargebacks
If something's wrong, please contact us first — we can almost always resolve it faster than a
chargeback, and a chargeback may suspend your account while it's investigated.

Questions: [support@gtmcat.xyz] · GTMCat, San Francisco, CA, USA.
